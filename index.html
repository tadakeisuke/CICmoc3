DESCRIPTION

Immerse yourself in one of Japan's three great traditional Bon dances. Experience the captivating Awa Odori festival that lights up Tokushima's summer nights. Embodying the spirit of "You're a fool whether you dance or watch, so you might as well dance!", spectators are invited to join the dancing circles. Enjoy the spectacular sight of colorfully dressed yukata-clad dancers parading through the streets, accompanied by live performances of traditional instruments including taiko drums, shamisen, and flutes.
